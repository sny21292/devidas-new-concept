About Davidas Design Concepts

Our Story

Our Beginnings

Davidas Design Concepts started as a company on July 8, 1995 as a repair service center for retail jewelry stores. After working many years as an employee for other jewelry stores and with other jewelry repair people, I became very un-happy and dissatisfied with the quality of work I saw.

The quality of repair work varied greatly between jewelry stores and was due to the skill level of the jewelry repairperson but also the equipment available to them as well as other factors. Many customers were turned away with jewelry repair request due to these conditions.

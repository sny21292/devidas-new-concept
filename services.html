Our Services

◆

Jewelry Repair

If you're looking for a shop that offers expert jewelry repair in Greensboro, NC, Davidas Design Concepts is the one you can trust. We operate one of the most complete and technologically advanced jewelry repair and manufacturing facilities in the entire state of North Carolina.

Our team specializes in custom jewelry design, using the latest innovations in CAD modeling, 3D printing, precision milling, and laser technology to bring your ideas to life with unmatched accuracy and artistry.

From everyday pieces to the most delicate heirlooms, we can repair silver, gold, platinum, and even gold-filled jewelry with exceptional craftsmanship. We are also one of the few shops in the region with the skill and equipment to restore antique filigree and intricate heirloom pieces — the kind of work most jewelers simply cannot do.

At Davidas Design Concepts, quality is not just a promise; it's the standard. Every repair, restoration, and custom project is handled with the highest level of care, precision, and integrity.

Contact us today to learn more about our jewelry repair and custom design services and discover why so many customers trust us with their most meaningful pieces.

◎

Jewelry Appraisals

We provide all types of appraisals on jewelry items, loose stones and watches. The types of appraisals we provide are insurance, liquidation, replacement and audit. We also offer stone mapping, sarin reports and re-cutting analysis.

✎

Engraving

We do all types of engraving from hand to machine and computer aided engraving. This enables us to engrave on glass and most metals. We also have the ability to take any picture or artwork you have and engrave it on your favorite mug, wine glass, charm or ring. We also engrave signs, name tags and corporate logos.

◇

Hand Engraving

Hand engraving is a creative artistic decoration and embellishment on precious metals. Every piece done by a hand engraver holds within itself some of the artistic creativity and imagination of the engraver. Hand engraving is like a fine painting that will last many generations.

●

Pearl & Bead Stringing

We do all types of re-stringing from simple to complex.

◆

Custom Jewelry Designs

We can hand fabricate one of a kind pieces in platinum, gold or silver. We can design your dream piece of jewelry with the latest technology called CAD (computer-aided design). CAD gives you the ability to see your new piece of jewelry from all sides and angles and participate in its final design. If you are looking for that unique one of a kind item, you will be pleased with our custom design capabilities.

▣

CAD/CAM Design

The term CAD/CAM is a shortening of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM). CAD gives the designer the flexibility to change or modify a design to meet customer's desires and show them their new jewelry item before it's made.

◎

Jewelry Consultations

Jewelry consultations can include, but not limited to review of other appraisals, critical examination of craftsmanship, quality, and artistry. We provide consultations for personal purchases, investment purchases, resale, and auction potential.

⌚

Watch & Clock Repair

We repair any type of watch from Rolex, Vacheron Constantin and Baume & Mercier to department store fashion watches. Our watchmaker is Rolex certified. We specialize in restoration of old and antique pocket watches. We also repair clocks from table clocks to grandfather and wall clocks including specialty and cuckoo clocks.
